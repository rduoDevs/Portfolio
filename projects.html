CameraService

Inspired by my aims to capture a bit more realism on Roblox, CameraService is an open-sourced alternative to Roblox's default game camera, allowing developers to customize mechanics such as smoothness, axis-locking, wobbling, and more. It supports both 2D & 3D games, and is based upon raycasting, interpolation, damping algorithms, and vector algebra. It's been downloaded by 500+ developers across Roblox Marketplace & GitHub, and earned a shoutout by a Roblox employee!
